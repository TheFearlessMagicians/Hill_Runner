How Hillrunner Works

The game consists of two players: the quest assigner and the hillrunner

Quest Assigner

Bruins in need can assign Hillrunners to deliver items to them. Whether be it groceries, lecture notes or lunch -- they got you covered. All you have to do is pay an affordable service fee and everything you want is in your fingertips!

Hillrunner

Want extra money? Be a Hillrunner! Hillrunners complete tasks assigned by quest assigners and in return, they get paid a reasonable amount through Venmo or cash. Also, Hillrunners can get rewards from the app as they level up!
